Large images were first encoded with bpgenc at q24 filesizes.
Medium is 60% of Large. Small is 60% of Medium. Tiny is 60% of Small. Everything else was matched to +/- 5% filesize.

All images have been subsampled to full range YCbCr prior to compression. All lossy images are encoded with chroma subsampling 4:2:0, except FLIF which does not support subsampling, and JPEG2000.
All lossless images are subsampled to YCbCr 4:4:4.

BPG, JPEG 2000, JPEG XR, and WebP decoded in javascript when needed. Rescaling is through Lanczos2.
